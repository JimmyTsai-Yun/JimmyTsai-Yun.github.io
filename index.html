  1. Multi-granular Crew Activity Recognition for Construction Monitoring
    Cheng-Yun Tsai, Mik Wanul Khosiin, Jacob J. Lin, Chuin-Shan Chen
    Automation in Construction (SCI) — November 2025
    A framework that jointly recognizes individual actions, identifies collaborating crews, and infers site-level activities on construction sites. We model workers as nodes in a graph and combine GNN with self-attention to fuse visual and spatial cues, achieving an overall F1 score of 70.31% on rebar, formwork, and concrete operations.
